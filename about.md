Welcome! I am a PhD student at Wharton, with interests in Behavioral Economics. 

In my current projects, I take beliefs seriously and apply them towards both asset pricing and household finance. Where possible, I like to combine the richness of alternative data with the structure of economic theory to answer questions fundamental to human (or machine) behavior.

Previously, I received my A.B. in Statistics from Harvard, with a Secondary in Computer Science. I also spent time working in Finance and with start-ups.
